CANVAS 2025 Presentation

November 2025

I had the opportunity to present my research, "Comparing Geostatistical and Machine Learning Modeling for the Creation of Soil Nutrient Management Zones in the Southeastern Coastal Plains" at the November 2025 CANVAS conference. I spoke on my findings in comparing geostatistical and machine learning techniques, as well as sampling methodology in the production of soil nutrient management zones in the Southeastern Coastal Plain. This was an amazing opportunity to have as my first international conference, and I am very grateful to be able to present.

Fieldwork in Perry, GA

April 2025

Excited to share that our lab's field work on the UGA Grand Farm Perry, Ga site has come to an end! After almost 2 years of day trips, the field has been completed for the collection of 75 soil profiles, and over 277 surficial soil samples.
